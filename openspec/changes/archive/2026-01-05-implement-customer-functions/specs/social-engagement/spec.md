## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Community Feedback
The system MUST allow customers to share their experience by leaving ratings and reviews for individual cars.

#### Scenario: Post Vehicle Review
- **WHEN** a logged-in user submits a 5-star rating with a comment
- **THEN** the review is saved and becomes visible to other public users on the car's detail page.

### Requirement: Social Proof Visibility
Vehicle detail pages MUST display aggregate rating information to help users make informed decisions.

#### Scenario: View Average Rating
- **WHEN** a car has multiple reviews
- **THEN** the detail page displays the average star rating.
